About

Built on decades of touring expertise, Marra helps creators, personalities, and brands create unforgettable live experiences.

As audiences shift from traditional media toward podcasts, creators, and community-driven brands, live entertainment is evolving faster than ever before. Success in this new landscape requires more than traditional touring knowledge—it requires an understanding of modern audiences and how to transform engaged communities into unforgettable live experiences.

Marra brings together decades of experience from the highest levels of global touring with hands-on expertise in the rapidly growing world of podcast, creator, and non-traditional entertainment. Collectively, our team has worked alongside some of the world's most influential artists, personalities, and media brands, including Fleetwood Mac, The Rolling Stones, Kendrick Lamar, Stevie Nicks, A$AP Rocky, Childish Gambino, Blink-182, and Netflix, as well as leading podcasts and personalities such as Call Her Daddy, Mel Robbins, and My Favorite Murder.

Our work begins long before the doors open. From concept development and strategy to production and execution, we help shape experiences from idea to reality.

Built on decades of touring expertise and shaped by the future of entertainment, Marra helps communities come together in the real world.
